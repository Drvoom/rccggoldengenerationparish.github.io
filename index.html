Testimonials

Morijorch

Brothers and sisters, I stand before you today to share the incredible journey of faith that has brought me to this point. I was lost and uncertain, burdened by the challenges of life. But through the grace of God, I found my way to this loving community. I felt His presence in every step I took, guiding me through the darkest moments and lifting me up in times of need. This church has been my refuge, a place where I've grown closer to God and forged deep connections with fellow believers. Today, I testify to the transformative power of faith, the strength of unity, and the unwavering love of our Creator. May we continue to support and uplift one another as we walk this path of faith together.

Rochak

Dear brothers and sisters, I want to share how God's healing touch has brought restoration to my life. A few years ago, I was battling a debilitating illness that seemed insurmountable. Doctors were uncertain about my prognosis, and fear gripped my heart. But through the fervent prayers of this church family, I witnessed the miraculous hand of God at work. Slowly but surely, my health began to improve. It was as if His divine light was dispelling the darkness of illness. Today, I stand before you as living proof that God is the ultimate healer, and this church is a testament to the power of communal faith. Let us continue to believe in His healing grace and extend our love and support to those in need.

Brad Johns

Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior! I want to share my testimony of how God's provision has carried me through times of financial hardship. A few years ago, I lost my job unexpectedly, and my family faced uncertainty regarding our future. But we clung to our faith and trusted that God would provide for us. Through the kindness of this church community, we received assistance that helped us meet our basic needs. Additionally, I received a job opportunity that not only sustained us but also allowed us to give back to others in need. This experience has taught me the value of faith in action and the importance of being part of a caring church family. Let us continue to support one another and be instruments of God's provision and generosity.
